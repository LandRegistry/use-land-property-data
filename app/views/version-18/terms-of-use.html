HM Land Registry’s Use land and property data Terms of use

By using this service, you accept these terms of use.

About the service

Use this service to access HM Land Registry's datasets about all land and property in England and Wales. Each dataset is listed separately with details of the different formats available, how often datasets are updated, and when they were last updated. Some may need specialist software to download them.

For some of the datasets you’ll need to agree a separate licence.

These datasets all contain large amounts of data so if you’re looking for information about one address or a few addresses, you can use other HM Land Registry services to find this information: https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/land-registry

Creating an account

You’ll need to create an account for some datasets. We’ll tell you if you need an account.

There are 3 different types of account depending on who you are and how you plan to use the data.

You can create an account for your:

  • own use
  • work in a UK company or charity
  • work in an overseas company

You’ll need to give different information depending on the type of account.

We may ask for your credit or debit card details. We will not take any payment or record your card details. We ask for this information to identify you.

Accounts for own use are created immediately. All other accounts will take up to one working day to be approved. If we receive an application for an account at the end of the working day or over the weekend, we’ll complete it on the next working day.

You cannot change your details once you create an account.

If you need to change your name or address details, you can set up a new account using a different email address.

Keeping your account secure

You are responsible for:

  • keeping your username and password secure
  • keeping your API key secure. Your API key grants full access to your account and should be protected the same way you would protect your password
  • all activity that occurs on or through your account

If you think your account security has been breached, contact HM Land Registry.

You can:

  • telephone 0300 006 0411
  • speak to a Welsh speaker on 0300 006 0422
  • email data.services@mail.landregistry.gov.uk

HM Land Registry is not responsible for any losses if someone without authorisation uses your account.

If you do not use your account for 12 months, it will be deleted. This is a security measure to protect you and your information. If you want to start using the service again, you’ll need to re-register.

Account closure

Your account will remain live to enable you to use the service at any time. You can ask for your account to be closed and we’ll de-activate your account.

To close your account, you can:

  • call HM Land Registry on 0300 006 0411
  • speak to a Welsh speaker, call 0300 006 0422
  • email data.services@mail.landregistry.gov.uk

We will not delete personal data where it is required to establish, defend, or exercise legal claims, for example in relation to property or registration fraud. Personal information will be retained for a minimum period of five years.

  • allow anyone else access your account
  • claim any arrangement or official partnership with HM Land Registry, Royal Mail, and Ordnance Survey, to supply or re-use information

If you are not using this service in the agreed way, HM Land Registry can end your use of the service and cancel your account at any time.

Your information and how we use it

If you do not set up an account, the only information we hold is your IP address. If you create an account, we will hold your:

  • name
  • address
  • telephone number
  • company or charity details (if provided)
  • email address
  • password
  • contact preferences
  • IP address

If HM Land Registry needs to contact you, we’ll email you using the email address you provided.

How we protect your information

HM Land Registry follows the Data Protection Legislation and European Parliament Directives.

This means that HM Land Registry uses the personal information you provide in accordance with its personal information charter to:

  • create and manage your account
  • provide the data files you request
  • record the download
  • contact you
  • prevent and detect crime

HM Land Registry only shares your personal information with other organisations if we are allowed to by law. We will:

  • make sure nobody has access to it who shouldn’t
  • only keep the information for as long as it’s needed
  • not make it available for commercial use, such as marketing, without your permission

If we need help processing your request for land and property data, your information can be shared with other:

  • teams in HM Land Registry
  • organisations

There are some cases when your information can be shared for other reasons, for example to prevent crime.

How to make a complaint

If you’re unhappy with the service, contact us.

You can:

  • email data.services@mail.landregistry.gov.uk.
  • telephone 0300 006 0411
  • speak to a Welsh speaker 0300 003 0422

If you’re unhappy with the answer you receive, follow the HM Land Registry’s internal complaints procedure.

If you’re still unhappy with the answer you can contact the Information Commissioner at https://ico.org.uk/.
