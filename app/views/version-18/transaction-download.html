Download Transaction Data

Data about the number and types of applications HM Land Registry complete each month.

The dataset is available to access on the 15th working day of the month and contains information from the previous month. For example, files published in April show the transactions we completed in March.

By downloading this dataset, you are agreeing to the OGL conditions of use.
